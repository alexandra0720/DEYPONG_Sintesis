    Although the outcome of the latest crises has been very different from that taken in 2008, the truth is that poverty and social exclusion continue to be a pending problem that affects 12.3 million people in our country, according to data from the XIII Report 'The State of Poverty in Spain' by EAPN-ES.

    The cost of housing, the gender gap and insufficient support for families with children have become key factors in generating poverty in Spain. The recovery of families is being hindered by the effects of inflation, the price of energy, and constantly rising rents and mortgages, which prevent millions of people from having a life in decent conditions.
